## Q3 (Month 7-9): Scale or Pivot

### Month 7: Decision Point
Option A: Commercial success -> FDA pathway
If: 1+ paying customer, metrics good, clear pipeline.

Next steps:
- Start ISO 13485 QMS preparation
- Draft clinical trial protocol
- Identify trial partner hospitals
- Budget: 6-12M THB over 18 months

Option B: Partial success -> refine and scale
If: 1 pilot converted, slow sales, pricing issues.

Next steps:
- Focus on customer success
- Optimize product based on feedback
- Delay FDA pathway to Year 2
- Budget: Continue at current burn

Option C: Pivot required
If: No paying customers, fundamental issues.

Next steps:
- Root cause analysis
- Evaluate strategic options:
  - Different customer segment
  - Different value proposition
  - Partner/acquisition
  - Graceful shutdown

### Month 8-9: Execute Decision
If FDA pathway:
- [ ] QMS documentation started
- [ ] Clinical protocol drafted
- [ ] IRB submission prepared
- [ ] Trial budget finalized

If refine and scale:
- [ ] Product improvements based on feedback
- [ ] Sales process optimization
- [ ] Expand pilot to 2-3 more sites
- [ ] Marketing campaigns launched
